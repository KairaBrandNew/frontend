Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on 18-02-2025

Policy Overview

DURAI KARTHICK P L believes in helping its customers as far as possible and has a liberal cancellation policy.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations will be considered only if requested immediately after placing the order.
  • Orders that have been processed for shipping cannot be canceled.
  • Cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers and eatables are not accepted.
  • Refunds/replacements can be made if the customer proves poor product quality.

Refund Policy

  • Damaged or defective items must be reported within 2 days of receipt.
  • Complaints about product mismatch must be reported within 2 days.
  • Products with manufacturer warranties should be directed to the manufacturer.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 1-2 business days.
